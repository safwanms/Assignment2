Veg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Water
  • Carrot
  • Beans
  • Cabbage

Process

  1. Rinse rice very well till the water runs clear of starch. Soak rice in water for 30 mins
  2. Add the soaked and drained rice to the hot water
  3. When the cooked rice is cooling, chop the veggies finely and keep aside
  4. Add the garlic, ginger and saut√© for some seconds. No need to brown the garlic
  5. Add the soy sauce, salt and pepper. Stir quickly and add cooked and cooled rice
  6. Stir fry for a few minutes until the sauce has coated the rice well
  7. Serve the veg fried rice hot as it is or with with your favorite Chinese side
