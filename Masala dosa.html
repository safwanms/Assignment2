Masala Dosa

Ingredients


  • Idli Rice
  • Chana Dal
  • Urad Dal
  • Fenugreek Seeds(Methi)
  • Water
  • Salt

Process

  1. Lightly stir the batter, before you begin to make dosa
  2. Heat a cast iron pan or a flat non-stick pan. The pan should be medium hot
  3. Spread the batter in a circular way on the pan
  4. Sprinkle some oil on top and edges
  5. Cover with a lid and let it cook
  6. When you see the base has become golden and the edges separate from the pan
