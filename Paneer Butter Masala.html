Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Cashews
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Garam Masala

Process

  1. Soak 18 to 20 cashews in ⅓ cup hot water for 20 to 30 minutes
  2. make the ginger garlic paste
  3. add 2 cups of diced or roughly chopped tomatoes
  4. Add the prepared crushed ginger-garlic or 1 teaspoon ready-made ginger-garlic paste
  5. Begin to cook the tomato purée on a low to medium-low heat
  6. After the gravy thickens to your desired consistency, then add the paneer cubes
  7. Stir gently but well. If you haven’t yet, switch off the heat
